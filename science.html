CSIC

CSIC (Consejo Superior de Investigaciones Científicas), is the main public research body in Spain. Assigned to the Ministry of Education and Science, the CSIC has a multidisciplinary character and carries out research in all fields of science thanks to more than a hundred centers spread throughout Spain.
